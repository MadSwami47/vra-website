For the property itself

For the property itself.

Multifamily isn't one risk — it's a stack. Building exposure, third-party liability in common areas, the management entity behind the property, and the boards and committees that govern it.

We structure the program so the limits, deductibles, and lender requirements all line up. No coverage gap between the property policy and the entity protecting the ownership.

Lender-grade requirements

  • ✓ Replacement-cost property with LOR
  • ✓ $1M GL with $5M – $25M umbrella
  • ✓ Waiver of subrogation to lender
  • ✓ Ordinance or law (A, B, and C)
  • ✓ Fidelity bonds for HOA boards
  • ✓ D&O for management entities
