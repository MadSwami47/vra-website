What's included

The multifamily & HOA coverage stack.

A program built for multifamily owners and HOA-governed communities. Each line is sized to the property and the way it is run.

#1

Property

Buildings, common areas, fixtures, and FF&E. Sized to the community — owner-operated complexes and HOA-managed properties.

#2

Equipment Breakdown

HVAC, boilers, elevators, and electrical systems — common-area failures standard property won't respond to.

#3

General Liability

Tenant and visitor injury — stair slips, parking-lot incidents, pool and common-area claims.

#4

Loss of Rents

Income replacement when units are uninhabitable after fire, storm, or system failure. Up to 24 months.

#5

D&O (HOA Boards)

Board-member liability for governance decisions, owner disputes, and fiduciary claims.

#6

Crime / Fidelity

Theft of funds by employees, vendors, or board members — critical for HOA reserves and operating accounts.

#7

Workers Comp

State-mandated. Covers onsite maintenance, leasing, and groundskeeping staff at owner-operated properties.

#8

Umbrella / Excess

Additional limit stacked above your primary policies. The catastrophic-claim layer for the community.
