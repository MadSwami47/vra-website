Why it matters

Independent broker vs captive sales channel.

The structural difference between how an independent broker and a captive agent work for you — and against you.

An independent advisor

What you get at VRA.

  • ✓ Quotes from multiple A-rated carriers, side-by-side
  • ✓ Industry-specialist coverage built by vertical
  • ✓ Re-shopping at every renewal, not just year one
  • ✓ A broker who advocates with the carrier at claim time
  • ✓ Transparent pricing and commission structure
  • ✓ Coverage gaps surfaced, not glossed over
A captive sales channel

What you usually get elsewhere.

  • — A single house quote with limited comparison
  • — Packaged BOPs that miss industry-specific exposure
  • — Renewal auto-quoted from the same carrier
  • — Claims handled by the carrier, not the agent
  • — Pricing tied to carrier production targets
  • — Gaps the policyholder finds out about at claim time
