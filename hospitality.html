What's included

The hospitality coverage stack.

What we put together when we quote a hotel. Each line is sized to the property — not bundled as a one-size template.

#1

Property

Building, FF&E, contents, equipment, and signage. Sized to the property — single location to portfolio.

#2

Business Interruption

Income replacement when fire, storm, or system failure forces the property offline. Up to 12 months.

#3

General Liability

Premises claims and third-party injury — guest slips, pool incidents, parking-lot claims.

#4

Liquor Liability

Dram-shop claims tied to alcohol service — a separate trigger from GL, required where you serve.

#5

Cyber & Data

Breach response, ransomware, PCI fines, and system downtime. Hospitality is a top-three target sector.

#6

Workers Comp

State-mandated. Covers staff injuries — housekeeping, F&B, maintenance, front-of-house.

#7

EPLI

Employment-practices liability — harassment, wrongful-termination, and discrimination claims.

#8

Umbrella / Excess

Additional limit stacked above your primary policies. The catastrophic-claim layer.
