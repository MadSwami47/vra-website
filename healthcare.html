What's included

The healthcare coverage stack.

A standard BOP will not respond to a malpractice suit. We build a layered program covering the licensed work, the space it happens in, and the people inside.

#1

Medical Malpractice

Patient-harm claims and the legal defense behind them. Tailored to specialty — physicians, dentists, CRNAs, anesthesia, and allied health.

#2

License defense

Board complaints and licensing-body investigations — a separate trigger from malpractice.

#3

Owned/Leased Property

Buildings, equipment, fixtures, and records — sized to fit whether you own or lease the space.

#4

General Liability

Premises claims and third-party injury — the visitor slip, the waiting-room incident.

#5

Business interruption

Income replacement when an event forces a temporary closure of the practice.

#6

Cyber & HIPAA

Breach response, ransomware, regulatory fines, and the notification costs that follow.

#7

Workers comp

State-mandated. Covers staff injuries — needlestick, lift, slip, and repetitive-strain claims.

#8

EPLI

Employment-practices liability — harassment, wrongful-termination, and discrimination claims.
